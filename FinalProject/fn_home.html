About

Welcome to the Fortnite Championship Series Official Website! The championship series is a tournament that offers the highest level of Fortnite with a prize pool that only the best of the best can compete for. In this tournament there will be a constant leaderboard update that will provide statistics of who is at the top. This championship series is dedicated to showing the world who are the most dominant players. In the navigation pane you will find the leaderboard, stream, compete, and contact tabs to discover more about the FNCS.
